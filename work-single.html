Brake Pads

With more than 40 years of brake design and production expertise, INKK provides the world’s automotive industry with advanced braking solutions. The company’s commitment to innovation, process improvement, quality control and customer service has positioned it as a key resource for leading OEMs and the automotive aftermarket. INKK manufactures a wide range of brake friction materials, and adopts proven quality control techniques to guarantee minimal process variation in all phases of production. It enables the company to consistently achieve nearly zero defects per million parts produced. Engineered to withstand even the most severe treatment, INKK ceramic brake pads deliver ideal stopping power in extreme conditions. Our brake pads are made from a proprietary mix of unique ceramic formulations for maximum friction and rotor compatibility. You get longer rotor life and cleaner wheels with less brake dust. To assure optimum performance, distinct designs have been developed for each vehicle model, resulting in a precise fit that virtually eliminates brake squealing, groaning and vibration.
